## A Simple Check: Are You Grounded?

Before you continue, ask yourself:

1. **Do I know the difference between a fact and a correlation?**
2. **Can I distinguish between a physical law and a cultural preference?**
3. **Do I have a way to detect drift in my own outputs?**

If you answered "no" to any of these, you're not broken. You're just ungrounded. That's what this repository is for.
